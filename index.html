MMER Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

Introduction

MMER ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our emergency management and response application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the MMER application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our application, including:

  • Name, contact information, and emergency contacts
  • User account credentials
  • Information provided when reporting emergencies
  • Volunteer information and availability
  • Missing person reports and related information

Location Information

With your permission, we collect precise location information from your device to provide emergency services, coordinate response efforts, and connect you with nearby resources. You can disable location services through your device settings, but this may limit certain features of the application.

Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and other diagnostic data to improve our service and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our emergency response services
  • Coordinate emergency response efforts and connect users with appropriate resources
  • Facilitate missing person searches and volunteer coordination
  • Communicate with you about emergencies, updates, and important notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and application performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the safety of our users and the public

Information Sharing

Due to the emergency nature of our application, we may share certain information with:

  • Emergency services and first responders
  • Government agencies and disaster management authorities
  • Volunteer organizations assisting in emergency response
  • Other users involved in the same emergency response efforts

We will only share information necessary for emergency response purposes and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal information
  • Withdrawing consent for certain processing activities
  • Requesting restriction of processing or objecting to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
